# Research Paper Summary

This project is grounded in foundational LLM and tokenization research.

## Key Papers

- Sennrich et al. (2016): BPE for NMT
- Schuster & Nakajima (2012): WordPiece
- Kudo (2018): SentencePiece
- Brown et al. (2020): GPT-3
- Touvron et al. (2023): LLaMA

These works establish tokenization as a core architectural decision.
